1、the

1) out of question 没问题

out of the question 不可能

2）take place 发生了

Greate changes have taken place here.

take the place of 替代

Now plastics have taken the place of many materials.

3) in charge of 管理

Smith is in charge of our class.

in the charge of 被管理

Our class is in the charge of Smith.

4) in possession of 拥有

The old man is in possession of a large fortune .

in the possession of 被拥有

Large fortune is in the possession of the old man。

2、in and in the

1.at table 在吃饭

at the table 在桌子旁

2.at school 在上课，在求学

at the school 在学校里

3.by sea 走海路，乘船

by the sea 在海边

4.by hand 用手工

by the hand 搀着，拉着手

5.by day 在白天

by the day 按日计算

6.in red 穿着红色衣服

in the red 赤字；亏损

7.in train 准备就绪

in the train 在火车上

8.in front of 在…（外部）的前面，前面

in the front of 在…(内部)的前面，前部

9.in office 执政

in the office 在办公室里

10.in hospital/prison 住院/坐牢

in the hospital/prison 在医院里/在监狱里

11.in charge of 负责

in the charge of 由…负责

12.in place of 代替

in the place of 在…地方

13.in future 从今往后

in the future 在将来
